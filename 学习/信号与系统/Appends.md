# Appends

* **二元方程组**

$\begin{cases}
    A_1x + B_1y = C_1 \\
    A_2x + B_2y = C_2
\end{cases}$

$\begin{cases}
    D = \begin{vmatrix} A_1 & B_1 \\ A_2 & B_2 \\\end{vmatrix} \\
    \\
    x = - \begin{vmatrix} B_1 & C_1 \\ B_2 & C_2 \\\end{vmatrix} / D \\
    \\
    y = \begin{vmatrix} A_1 & C_1 \\ A_2 & C_2 \\\end{vmatrix} / D \\
\end{cases}$
